About Us

Welcome to Pole Star, Madhubani an English medium day/boarding co-ed CBSE 10+2 school established by a registered socio cultural organization Vikas Bandhu, giving the true meaning of its name has been shining in the heart of Mithilanchal since last 17th years.

It believes that learning is a joyful experience which should be based on moral, spiritual ethical and secular values derived from the glorious Indian culture and heritage. The ideals of the institution reflects harmonious blend of ancient and Modern Methodology and pedagogy.
